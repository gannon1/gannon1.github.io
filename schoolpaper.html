School Shootings White Paper

Project Summary

The goal of this paper was to find a problem in the world that has no obvious solution and research ways to make the issue less significant.

Skills Learned

  • Ability to effectively present ideas and solutions in a written form
  • Ability to research and understand information for a topic

Challenges

Research for my topic was my biggest challenge with this paper. A lot of the sources I found were simply analyses of school shootings rather than potential solutions to the problem. Because of that I had to broaden the area that I was searching in and look for sources that weren't specifically about school shootings.
